Pizza Dough

Desription

This pizza dough recipe is so easy you'll never be buying storebought again!

Ingredients

  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 packet instant yeast
  • 1 12 oz beer
  • 1 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt and yeast in large bowl.
  2. Pour in beer and olive oil. Mix until well combined. Keep adding flour until dough is coming together as a ball and not too sticky.
  3. Let rest for 1-2 hours. Should double in size.
  4. Roll out on floured surface. Add toppings. Drizzle more olive oil around crust edges. Bake at 425 for 11-15 minutes.
